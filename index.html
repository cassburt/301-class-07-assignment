Here's a little bit about me

Born to poor, wayward gypsies, I was raised by a pack of marmuts when my parents were killed in a felting accident.
After reurning to civilization, I helped a local village by acting as a water diviner during the massive drought of 1992 (I was lucky, really; don't know the first thing about water divination).
And, long story short, now I make websites.
